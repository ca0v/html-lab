<wbr>
The <wbr> tag is used to specify where in a word a line break may be added if the text is too long to fit in its containing element. The <wbr> tag is useful when you do not want to use CSS to control the width of text. Here is a long word that breaks in convenient places: Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis
